ECO-5007A: Intermediate Microeconomics

By LET

Skills Workshops

Rearranging Equations

Master the fundamental algebraic techniques to isolate variables and solve equations, a crucial skill for all quantitative analysis.

Differentiation Rules

Explore the basic rules of differentiation, including power, constant, sum, and difference rules, essential for understanding rates of change.

Differentiation Chain Rule

Learn how to differentiate composite functions using the chain rule, enabling you to tackle more complex derivatives.

Differentiation Product Rule

Understand how to differentiate functions that are products of two or more terms using the product rule.

One Variable Optimisation

Discover how to find maximum and minimum points of functions with a single variable, a core concept in economic optimization.

Partial Differentiation

Extend differentiation to functions with multiple variables, allowing you to analyze the impact of one variable while holding others constant.

Constrained Optimisation

Learn to optimize functions subject to constraints using methods like the Lagrangian, vital for understanding consumer and firm behavior.

Subject Specific Topics

Marshallian Demand

Understand how consumers make choices to maximize utility given their budget, leading to the derivation of Marshallian demand functions.
