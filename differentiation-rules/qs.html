Power Rule

Given the function $y=ax^n$, the derivative is:

$$ \frac{dy}{dx}=nax^{n-1} $$

Use this rule to differentiate:

1. $y=5x^4$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 20x^3 $$

2. $y=7x^2$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 14x $$

3. $y=\frac{2}{3}x^6$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 4x^5 $$

These indices facts will be useful for the next questions:

$$ x^{-n} = \frac{1}{x^n} $$ $$ x^{\frac{1}{n}} = \sqrt[n]{x} $$ $$ x^a \times x^b = x^{a+b} $$ $$ \frac{x^a}{x^b} = x^{a-b} $$ $$ (x^a)^b = x^{ab} $$

Use the power rule to differentiate:

4. $y=\frac{3x^3}{x^5}$

Show Solution

$y = 3x^{-2}$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = -6x^{-3} $$

5. $y=\frac{10}{x^2}$

Show Solution

$y = 10x^{-2}$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = -20x^{-3} $$

6. $y=2x^{\frac{2}{3}}$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{4}{3}x^{-\frac{1}{3}} $$

7. $y=4\sqrt{x}$

Show Solution

$y = 4x^{1/2}$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 2x^{-1/2} $$

8. $y=\frac{2}{3x^2}$

Show Solution

$y = \frac{2}{3}x^{-2}$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = -\frac{4}{3}x^{-3} $$

9. $y=\frac{10}{x}$

Show Solution

$y = 10x^{-1}$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = -10x^{-2} $$

The power rule can be used to show:

$$ y=ax, \quad \frac{dy}{dx}=a $$ $$ y=a, \quad \frac{dy}{dx}=0 $$

Put this altogether and differentiate:

10. $y=5x^2+4x+3$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 10x + 4 $$

11. $y=x^4-2x^{-5}+\frac{1}{x}$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 4x^3 + 10x^{-6} - x^{-2} $$

12. $y=1+2\sqrt{x}+\frac{2}{5x}+\frac{3}{4x^2}+\frac{5}{2x^3}$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = x^{-1/2} - \frac{2}{5}x^{-2} - \frac{3}{2}x^{-3} - \frac{15}{2}x^{-4} $$

Exponentials

Given the function $y=ae^x$, the derivative is:

$$ \frac{dy}{dx}=ae^x $$

Not many questions here, differentiate:

1. $y=5e^x$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 5e^x $$

Logarithms

Given the function $y=a\ln x$, the derivative is:

$$ \frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{a}{x} $$

Differentiate:

1. $y=5\ln x$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{5}{x} $$

2. $y=\frac{\ln x}{2}-3e^x$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{2x} - 3e^x $$

The following log laws will be useful:

$$ \ln(AB)=\ln A+\ln B $$ $$ \ln\left(\frac{A}{B}\right)=\ln A-\ln B $$ $$ \ln(A^n)=n\ln A $$

Differentiate:

3. $y=\ln 4x$

Show Solution

$y=\ln 4 + \ln x$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{x} $$

4. $y=\ln 0.443x$

Show Solution

$y=\ln 0.443 + \ln x$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{x} $$

5. $y=\ln \frac{2}{x}$

Show Solution

$y=\ln 2 - \ln x$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = -\frac{1}{x} $$

6. $y=\ln x^{10}$

Show Solution

$y=10\ln x$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{10}{x} $$

7. $y=6\ln \frac{1}{2}x$

Show Solution

$y=6(\ln \tfrac{1}{2} + \ln x)$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{6}{x} $$

Mixed Questions

For each question find the requested derivative:

1. $y=5x^3+x^2-4x+1+3e^x+\ln 2x \quad \frac{dy}{dx}=$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 15x^2 + 2x - 4 + 3e^x + \frac{1}{x} $$

2. $y=2x+5\sqrt{x}-\frac{4}{x}+\frac{3}{x^2} \quad \frac{dy}{dx}=$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 2 + \frac{5}{2\sqrt{x}} + \frac{4}{x^2} - \frac{6}{x^3} $$

3. $y=5t^2-2t^{\frac{3}{4}}+6\ln 0.1t \quad \frac{dy}{dt}=$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dt} = 10t - \frac{3}{2}t^{-1/4} + \frac{6}{t} $$

4. $P=6e^n-4+\frac{5}{2}n-\frac{6}{\sqrt{n}} \quad \frac{dP}{dn}=$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dP}{dn} = 6e^n + \frac{5}{2} + 3n^{-3/2} $$

5. $G=5s^2(3s-4) \quad \frac{dG}{ds}=$

Show Solution

$G = 15s^3 - 20s^2$

$$ \frac{dG}{ds} = 45s^2 - 40s $$

By substituting into the function and into the derived function, find the value of the function and the value of the gradient at the given $x$ values:

6. $y=5x^2-12x+2, \quad x=2$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 10x - 12 $$

$y = 0$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 8 $$

7. $y=4x^3+x+10, \quad x=-1$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 12x^2 + 1 $$

$y = 5$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 13 $$

8. $y=x^2+5x-1, \quad x=-2.5$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 2x + 5 $$

$y = -7.25$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 0 $$

9. $y=4e^x-10\sqrt{x}, \quad x=2$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = 4e^x - \frac{5}{\sqrt{x}} $$

$y \approx 15.42$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} \approx 26.02 $$

10. $y=5-\frac{5}{x}+\frac{5}{x^2}, \quad x=1$

Show Solution
$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{5}{x^2} - \frac{10}{x^3} $$

$y = 5$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = -5 $$
